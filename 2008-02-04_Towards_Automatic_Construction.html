Towards Automatic Construction of Reusable Prediction Models for Component-Based Performance Engineering

Feb 4, 2008 ·  The paper about my Master's Thesis, doing static code analysis for Java.

T. Kappler, H. Koziolek, K. Krogmann, and R. H. Reussner. Towards Automatic Construction of Reusable Prediction Models for Component-Based Performance Engineering. In Proc. Software Engineering 2008, ser. LNI, K. Herrmann and B. Brügge, editors, vol. 121, Munich, Germany, Feb. 18-22, 2008, pp. 140–154.

Accepted for the Software Engineering Conference Munich 2008 (SE08).

Many thanks to my co-authors for making that happen, and especially to Prof. Dr. Reussner and Dr. Heiko Koziolek for the great mentoring, and for letting me write the thesis at IIT Delhi.
