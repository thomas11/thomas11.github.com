Publications

Most of the publications listed here are in text mining and ontology design. I worked on this at Karlsruhe Institute of Technology under René Witte. I owe him much gratitude for letting me get involved in this research as an undergrad and coaching me through my first contributions. My involvement in the publications from 2008 onward was minimal, they build on earlier work I was involved in.
