Mini-Bio

I studied Computer Science at KIT, Karlsruhe, Germany, writing my thesis at IIT Delhi, India. After graduating in 2007, I worked on a freelance project in linguistics with University of Heidelberg.

In 2008 I joined the Swiss Institute of Bioinformatics in Geneva, Switzerland and had a fantastic time developing tools for biologists, from web apps to storage backends to Semantic Web to Perl modules.

In 2011 I moved to Zurich, first developing medical applications at Nhumi Technologies, then continuing this work on a federally funded data warehousing research project with Nhumi and the zhaw college.

In 2013 I joined Microsoft Zurich, working on the Java + Linux + Azure backend of Social Engagement.

In 2016 I transferred to Azure Search in Redmond near Seattle to work on backend and search.

My LinkedIn page has the details.
